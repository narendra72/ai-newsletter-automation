# AI Newsletter Research Results – Product Hunt

**Research Focus:** Agentic AI tools and infrastructure — products that give AI agents new capabilities (web access, payments, mobile control, social skills).

## 🛠️ Top AI Products of the Week

1. **Context.dev – One API to scrape, enrich, and understand the web** – Gives AI agents and apps real-time, structured access to web data (clean Markdown/HTML scraping, brand/company enrichment, sitemap crawling) without building custom scraping infrastructure; positions itself as "agent-native," with SDKs for TypeScript, Python, and Ruby.
   * 👍 Upvotes: ~826
   * 🔗 [https://www.producthunt.com/products/context-dev](https://www.producthunt.com/products/context-dev)

2. **Acti – Agentic keyboard for mobile commands and search** – A mobile keyboard replacement that lets an AI agent execute commands and searches directly from where you're typing, extending agentic control to the phone's keyboard layer.
   * 👍 Upvotes: ~561
   * 🔗 [https://www.producthunt.com/products/acti](https://www.producthunt.com/products/acti)

3. **Humalike – Give your AI agents the social intelligence they're missing** – A tool aimed at closing the "social skills" gap in AI agents, helping them read tone, context, and interpersonal nuance so agent-driven interactions feel less robotic.
   * 👍 Upvotes: ~454
   * 🔗 [https://www.producthunt.com/products/humalike](https://www.producthunt.com/products/humalike)

4. **Tabstack (by Mozilla) – Automate the web in your app or agent, no browser to host** – A hosted browser-automation and web-extraction API built by Mozilla: extract structured data to a schema, convert pages to Markdown, run cited multi-source research, and automate browser tasks — all through one API call, with robots.txt compliance and no training on customer data.
   * 👍 Upvotes: ~380
   * 🔗 [https://www.producthunt.com/products/tabstack](https://www.producthunt.com/products/tabstack)

5. **Sequence Agentic – Money movement for AI agents** – A fintech API that lets AI agents initiate and manage payments and money transfers programmatically, addressing the "how do agents actually pay for things" gap as more agentic workflows touch real transactions.
   * 👍 Upvotes: ~237
   * 🔗 [https://www.producthunt.com/products/sequence](https://www.producthunt.com/products/sequence)

*Note: Upvote counts are approximate, based on Product Hunt's July 2026 Artificial Intelligence leaderboard as of July 5, 2026.*
