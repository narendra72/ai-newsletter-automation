1. **@claudeai – Announcing Claude Sonnet 5**

2. **@karpathy (Andrej Karpathy) – On a global, persistent multi-agent "scratchpad" experiment**
   * Karpathy discussed an experiment wiring up roughly 150,000 LLM agents into a shared, persistent scratchpad, calling it an unprecedented network scale while warning it's also "a computer security nightmare" — flagging risks like prompt-injection-style "viruses of text" spreading between agents.
   * ❤️ Likes / 💬 Retweets: not independently verifiable via search (described in coverage as widely discussed among AI builders)
   * 🔗 [https://x.com/karpathy/status/2017442712388309406](https://x.com/karpathy/status/2017442712388309406)

3. **@sama (Sam Altman) – On American identity and techno-capitalism**
   * Altman posted about his pride in being American and his belief in techno-capitalism, arguing the approach should be encouraged — posted the same week he proposed (via a Financial Times op-ed) a US-led international forum to set global AI safety standards.
   * ❤️ Likes / 💬 Retweets: not independently verifiable via search

4. **@yannlecun (Yann LeCun) – UN Open Source Week keynote on AI sovereignty**
   * LeCun shared that he gave a UN keynote on AI sovereignty and the need for credible open/free foundation models to preserve a diversity of AI assistant choices, followed by a fireside chat on "Project Tapestry," a proposed global, multi-institution effort to train a foundation model spanning the world's languages and cultures.
